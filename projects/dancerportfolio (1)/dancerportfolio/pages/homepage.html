Rana's Philosophy

Explores the body as a "corps-monde" (living territory) where emotions, memories, and identities are in perpetual transformation. Views dancing and choreography as inseparable, with movement stemming from sensation and choreography as a means to shape and share bodily discoveries. Focuses on reconnecting with the sensitive, vulnerable, and deeply human, creating spaces where personal experiences resonate collectively.

Rania has performed with renowned companies worldwide and now dedicates her artistry to creating unforgettable moments for weddings, corporate events, and private celebrations.
